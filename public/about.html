About Me

I'm a friendly and liberal person. Originally from the Bronx, New York, I worked in the medical field for 16 years. It started to become a challenge for me when I lost my hearing at age 27, but giving up wasn't an option so I received a cochlear implant, adjusted to my new life and pushed forward.
I needed a change of pace so I moved to Florida in 2014 and decided to pursue a different career path. I was 41 years old at the time and never in my life felt as determined to do something as I did with nails. I had a passion for it and knew I would enjoy this as a career. I've met and learned from some of the best in the industry and continue to grow my knowledge now.
My goal is to be the best Nail Specialist I can by using my experience and knowledge to make sure my clients always leave with a smile on their face.
